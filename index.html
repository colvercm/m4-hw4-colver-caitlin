Bio

Pink (P!nk) was born Alecia Beth Moore Hart on September 8, 1979 in Doylestown, Pennsylvannia. Her mother is Judith Moore, and her father is James Moore. Her parents divorced when she was 10 years old. She is of Jewish decent. Between the ages of 4 and 12 she was a competitive gymnast. By high school Pink had joined her first band called Middleground.

Personal Life

Pink is married to motocross racer Carey Hart. They married in 2006. They have two children; Jameson (7) and Willow (12).

Career

Pink started performing at the age of 14. By 16 years old, she form the girl group Choice with two other members. In 1995, LaFace Records saw potential in Pink and offered her a solo recording contract. Pink is regarded as “Pop Royalty” for her distinctive raspy voice and acrobatic stage presence. She has sold over 135 million records worldwide (60 million albums and 75 million singles), making her one of the world's best-selling music artists. In the 2000's, she was the second most played female-artist in the United Kingdom.
